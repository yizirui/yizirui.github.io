## Hiring-manager view

This work shows human-centered AI framing: designing AI systems around transparency, collaboration, and responsible use rather than only optimizing model output quality.

## Scientific problem

Instructional design is a collaborative human process. AI assistance has to be useful while keeping human goals, transparency, and accountability visible to educators.

## Method

- Contributed to a framework for responsible AI-supported instructional design.
- Studied how AI assistance can support collaboration without hiding decisions from human stakeholders.
- Connected system design choices to transparency and human-centered evaluation criteria.

## Evaluation signal

The project emphasizes whether AI assistance improves the design workflow while preserving transparency, responsibility, and meaningful human control.
